My first introduction to "design" was through an elective course I took during my undergrad titled "Human Factors for the Web" (before I took any architecture courses). It was through that course I learned to think about stakeholders, user-testing, and type and color. I didn't realize how much of an influence that one elective course had on my life until I began to reflect on what my interests really are and what I really want to do.
