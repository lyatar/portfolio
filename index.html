About Me

While working on my master's thesis, I found this diagram of how architects should also be hackers, data scientists, and algorithm builders. I saw it as my call to be an interdisciplinary practitioner.

My skill-set is a mix of architecture, design, data analysis, and research. I also have a strong interest in data visualization.

I'm currently located in the San Francisco Bay Area and I have degrees in Architecture, Comparative Literature, and Japanese. I enjoy making and building things and thinking about how it all comes together.
