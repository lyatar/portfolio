About Me

While working on my master's thesis, I found this diagram of how architects should also be hackers, data scientists, and algorithm builders.

Unknowingly, I'm on the path to be a data-informed designer.

I'm currently located in the San Francisco Bay Area and I have degrees in Architecture, Comparative Literature, and Japanese and many technical interests. While completing my Masters of Architecture at UC Berkeley, I rediscovered my passion for web design and coding and found a new one in data. I enjoy making and building things and thinking about how it all comes together.
